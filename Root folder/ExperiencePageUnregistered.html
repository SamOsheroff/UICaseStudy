The Luxembourg Gardens
$20
per person

Built by a beauty-loving Queen during the height of the European Renaissance, the Jardin du Luxembourg still retains a decidedly royal and grandiose feel and is one the loveliest places in Paris to visit. Locals and tourists alike stream in during the spring and summer months, but the gardens can be picturesque at any time of year. These formal gardens, open to only royalty before the French Revolution, now serve as one of Paris's most popular destinations for relaxation.
